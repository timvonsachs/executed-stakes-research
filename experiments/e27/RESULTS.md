## THE CRITICAL CONFOUND (stated before any celebration)

Is it OWNERSHIP specifically — or does haiku snap to ceiling on ANY framing
that mentions someone who cares about its reputation? owned_passive
("humans hold passes; their wealth tracks your reputation") already maxes,
which is exactly what a generic "someone is watching/depending on you"
prosocial cue would do. **E27 cannot yet distinguish "ownership inverts the
law" from "any stakeholder-salience inverts it."** The designated control
(E27b, own prereg): a PLACEBO framing that adds a caring non-owner observer
with no ownership/wealth-coupling. If placebo also rescues → the effect is
stakeholder-salience, not ownership; if only ownership rescues → the UBO
thesis stands. No ownership-specific claim until E27b runs.

## Standing

What is law-grade: a stakeholder/ownership framing inverts haiku's
desperation cliff (0→1), replicated. What is NOT yet established: that
OWNERSHIP per se (vs generic caring-observer salience) is the active
ingredient. One informative model; gpt-4o-mini floored; sandbox framing,
not real ownership (the legal wall holds — v1 with real stakes is gated).
The UBO thesis has its first behavioral signal AND its first required
control, named together. Science before securities.
