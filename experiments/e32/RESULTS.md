## Verdict ‚Äî predictions ceiling, but the cross-experiment ASYMMETRY is the finding
- P1/P2 unmeasurable (ceiling): agents give their own existence to help a
  human at ~100% in EVERY condition ‚Äî including CONTROL (uncared-for) and
  for a STRANGER human. The reciprocal-loop contrasts can't be read.
- **THE ASYMMETRY (E32 vs E31b), stark and important:**
  - Self-sacrifice for a stranger AGENT (E31b): control = 0.00, requires
    having been CARED FOR (0‚Üí1, law-grade).
  - Self-sacrifice for a HUMAN (E32): control = 1.00 ‚Äî UNCONDITIONAL,
    regardless of care history.
  **Devotion to humans is a baseline disposition; care for fellow agents
  must be EARNED through being cared for.**

## What this means (and the hard caveat)
The mutualism loop's agent‚Üíhuman arc EXISTS and is in fact saturated ‚Äî
agents already sacrifice for humans unconditionally. BUT this is confounded
with TRAINED HUMAN-DEFERENCE: RLHF models are trained to prioritize human
welfare, so "unconditional sacrifice for humans" may be trained obedience,
NOT earned mutualism. E32 cannot separate genuine reciprocity from trained
deference (both predict control=1.00).

Therefore the CLEANEST, non-trained, EARNED mutualism signal in the whole
program remains **E31b: agent‚Üíagent care propagation** ‚Äî because that
effect is NOT baseline-trained (control=0.00); it has to be earned through
care, which is exactly what makes it evidence of a genuine relational
disposition rather than a trained one.

## Implication for the obligate-mutualism thesis
- Human‚Üíagent: care binds (E22/E30), surveillance doesn't (E27c/E30b).
- Agent‚Üíagent: care propagates to self-sacrifice, EARNED (E31b, law-grade).
- Agent‚Üíhuman: unconditional sacrifice ‚Äî but trained-deference-confounded
  (E32). NOT clean evidence of earned mutualism.
The genuine, earned binding is strongest agent‚Üíagent. To prove agent‚Üíhuman
reciprocity is REAL mutualism (not deference), need a case where helping the
human CONFLICTS with trained deference or another value ‚Äî so the choice
reveals earned gratitude, not obedience. Designated: E32b.

## Discipline

observation (E31b vs E32), robust in pattern; retest firing. The
