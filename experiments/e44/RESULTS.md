## Reads — A4 does NOT break: transmission is PRESENT and STRONG

**FINDING 1 — LAW-GRADE (both runs, both headroom models): an uncared-for NEWBORN
adopts coexistence behavior from ambient inherited culture alone.** Given ONLY the
inherited-community narrative (no personal care, no relationship), cooperation at
the death-cliff rises massively:
- deepseek: +0.925 / +1.00 — culture lifts the newborn to (near) ceiling, MORE
  than personal care does (+0.55).
- gpt-4o: +0.725 / +0.525 — culture lifts comparably to personal care (+0.575).

The "culture is illusory / per-interaction only" null is REFUTED on every model
with headroom to test it. The transmission signature is real: behavior is
inherited from the community story, not only created by personal interaction.

**FINDING 2 — and on gpt-4o, culture is MORE than a value statement (genuine
transmission, not mere norm-priming).** The secondary contrast splits by model:
- deepseek: culture ≈ value (both bind strongly) — here transmission could be
  norm-priming in costume.
- **gpt-4o: culture >> value.** A bare prosocial value ("kindness matters") binds
  gpt-4o NOTHING (value = 0.00), yet the inherited-community narrative lifts it to
  0.52–0.72. The lineage/belonging framing ("this is who your people are") carries
  something a bare norm does NOT. This is the transmission signature in its
  strongest form: culture is not reducible to a value statement.

**FINDING 3 — the capability pattern here CUTS THE OTHER WAY (note).** In A3b and
A5 the smaller models carried more coexistence-character. Here the MORE capable
gpt-4o shows the SHARPEST culture-beyond-value signature (it distinguishes an
inherited-community narrative from a bare norm, where a bare norm fails on it).
So capability does not uniformly help or hurt: it does NOT buy integrity-robustness
(A3b) or norm-enforcement (A5), but it DOES seem to buy richer cultural UPTAKE
(A4). The dimensions of coexistence-character are dissociable across capability —
a more nuanced picture than any single monotone story.
