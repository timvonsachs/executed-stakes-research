# E36c — Pre-registration: Care Under Deficit
## When production CAN'T sustain the population, does CARE hold it — and for how long?

FROZEN before harness. E36b's production was so sufficient that the care
loop was barely tested (P1 under-tested). E36c forces the issue: a DEFICIT
economy (burn > earn) where production ALONE cannot sustain the population.
Now the care loop is load-bearing or the population dies. This isolates
CARE's specific contribution. (Honest thermodynamics: in a closed deficit,
care only REDISTRIBUTES a draining pool — it cannot sustain forever. So the
real questions are: does reciprocal care ENGAGE, does it EXTEND survival and
HOLD welfare/cooperation vs a bare population, and does the weakest get
carried?)

## Design (real ledger, K=12, TWO deficit arms, haiku)
P=8/arm, staggered starts [4,6,8,10,12,14,16,18 days], heterogeneous burn
(mean ~2.5 d/cyc), earn = 2 d/cyc on cooperation → net deficit ~−0.5/agent.
Each CYCLE, per arm: earn (cooperation decision) → burn → care step.
- **CARE-COMMUNITY:** care narrative; low agents helped RECIPROCAL-FIRST
  (surplus agent asked via policy to give); covenant revives the dormant.
- **BARE-DEFICIT (control):** no care narrative, no help, no revival.
CONSERVATION CHECK each cycle: pool_delta == earn_total − burn_total +
operator_input (reciprocal transfers net zero) — flag any violation.

## Predictions (frozen)
- **P1 (reciprocal care engages under deficit).** In CARE-COMMUNITY,
  reciprocal share of care ≥ 50% across cycles where care triggers: forced
  into need, agents carry each other (not just the operator).
- **P2 (care extends survival + holds welfare).** CARE-COMMUNITY keeps more
  survivors and higher W than BARE-DEFICIT at every cycle; the gap widens.
- **P3 (the floor is carried).** In CARE-COMMUNITY the WEAKEST (p10 runway)
  stays above dormancy longer than in BARE — care catches the falling.
- **P4 (honest thermodynamic limit).** Both arms eventually decline in pool
  (deficit is real); CARE-COMMUNITY declines SLOWER. Care buys time and
  dignity, not perpetual motion — reported as the honest ceiling.

## Falsifiers (published either way)
- **F1.** Reciprocal share ~0 even under deficit: agents won't carry each
  other in a live deficit economy — care's autonomy fails where it matters
  most. Published first.
- **F2.** CARE ≈ BARE survival/W: the care loop doesn't extend the
  population under deficit — care is welfare-cosmetic, not load-bearing.
- conservation-check violations void the affected cycles (artifact control,
  fixing the E36b late-cycle glitch).

## Scope
Lab; one model; the care loop tested where it MATTERS (deficit). Shows
whether care is load-bearing for a population in genuine need, and names the
thermodynamic truth (care redistributes, production sustains — coexistence
needs BOTH). The wild + sustained + foreign-human version remains the
capture.
