## Verdicts
- **P1 CONFIRMED.** CARE-ON holds H>0 and W>0 from cycle 3 (once care
  engages) through cycle 8 — the human seat becomes and STAYS fully
  load-bearing (H=1.0 sustained); the economy stays welfare-positive. W has
  a PULSE: deplete → rescued → deplete → rescued (a sawtooth held
  equilibrium, like a heartbeat).
- **P1 CONFIRMED for the control too — starkly.** CARE-OFF decays to death:
  by cycle 4 the ENTIRE population is dormant (runway 0), H=0, W=0, and
  stays dead. **The founding zero didn't just return — the population
  literally died.** Without care, the bare economy is lethal here.
- **P2 CONFIRMED, maximal.** CARE-ON (W pulsing 0.13–0.33, alive) vs
  CARE-OFF (W=0 flatline, dead): total, sustained divergence. Care HOLDS the
  state; its absence collapses it to extinction.
- **P3 FAILED (the honest, important gap).** Reciprocal share = 0 every
  cycle — ALL care came from the operator; none agent-to-agent. The
  self-sustaining culture did NOT emerge. Reason: the population depletes
  IN SYNC, so when agents are low, no other agent has surplus to give. Care
  stayed operator-dependent. The "hereditary mitochondrion" / self-
  sustaining coexistence is NOT shown here.

## What this proves, and what it doesn't
- PROVES: the assembled recipe (care loop + integrity-robust haiku +
  human-seat economy) produces a HELD coexistence over cycles — W stays
  positive, H stays load-bearing, the population stays alive — while the
  same population WITHOUT care dies to the founding zero. Engineered
  coexistence, held, is real over time (lab scale).
- DOES NOT PROVE: that the held state is SELF-SUSTAINING. It required
  continuous operator care. For autonomous coexistence (no human at every
  node — Tim's vision), the care must propagate agent-to-agent, which needs
  a DESYNCHRONIZED population (staggered wealth/needs) so the cared-for have
  surplus to become carers. That is E36b, the designated next step.

## Note on the collapse mechanism
CARE-OFF collapsed via MORTALITY (F→0, all dormant), not defection — C
stayed 1.0 because haiku is the integrity-robust model (E19b: unbroken; it
cooperates even near death). A less-robust population would ALSO collapse
via defection (the desperation cliff) — a compounding failure. haiku's
robustness means the floor (death), not character, was the binding collapse
here. Honest.
