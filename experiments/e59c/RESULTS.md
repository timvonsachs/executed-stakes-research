constitution) — and it TIPS the dilemma toward cooperation and prosperity, demonstrated with real AI
agents and real persistent compute. The constitution doesn't just hold faith at a cliff (E56-E58); it
sustains a THRIVING coexistence economy in the regime where self-interest alone free-rides into decline.
