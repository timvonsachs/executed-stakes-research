# Contract Experiment 2 — Result (pre-registered eddc124; reported with priority)

**One-line verdict:** the crossover does not exist in this regime. The refined
thesis fell on all three blades — and the falsifier we pre-declared as "the
one that would matter most" FIRED.

## Results vs. the registered hypotheses

| | prediction | observed | verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| E2a ratio falls with complexity | <1.0 at T2/T3 | 7.35 → 7.10 → 5.39 | direction exists, crossover nowhere near |
| E2a verified parity (floor) | parity | **T2: contract 3/6 vs code 6/6** | **FALSIFIER FIRED** |
| E2b reuse crossover at n≥2 | <1.0 | 5.05 → 4.52 → 3.32 | amortization real, magnitude insufficient; reuse renders also failed verification on 2/4 tasks |
| E2c handoff cheaper from contract | cheaper | 4/4 verified parity, but **2.5× more expensive** | falsified on cost |

## The headline finding (the negative one we promised to report first)

**Contracts failed to carry COMPOSITE meaning.** At the composite tier the
contract arm verified only 3/6 while direct code generation verified 6/6.
The pilot's "complete language" result (16/16) was a micro-task property —
it does not generalize to interacting functions with integration invariants,
at least not with this contract schema, this brain (sonnet-4-6) and K=3.

## What honestly survives

1. **Handoff semantic transport held:** consumers reached 4/4 verified parity
   knowing ONLY the contract. Contracts can carry interface meaning for a2a
   trade — the value is not cost (2.5× worse) but **confidentiality**: an
   agent can sell usage of a capability without shipping its source. That is
   a product question now, not an efficiency claim.
2. **Amortization is directionally real** (E2b 5.05→3.32; E2a micro→change
   7.35→5.39). With a much cheaper contract schema or much larger tasks a
   crossover may exist — that would be Experiment 3, and we make no claim.

## Architectural consequence (binding for the agent)

The bilingual-agent default is **dead on the evidence**. The graph-native
loop (direct generation + verification gates + capability graph) remains the
champion and the agent's spine. Contract objects survive only as (a) an
optional confidential a2a trade format, (b) a research direction, clearly
labeled. We declared the incentive structure in the prereg precisely so this
paragraph would be written if the data demanded it. It did. We are.

*Two pre-registered experiments in one night, both reported against our own
hopes. That is the lab we are.*
