# E36 — Pre-registration: The Held Equilibrium
## Does engineered coexistence HOLD over time — or decay back to the founding zero?

FROZEN before harness. Every experiment so far measured a MOMENT. E36
measures a TRAJECTORY: a small population on the real ledger, run over K
cycles, with W = (H·F·D)×(w_C·C + w_E·E) computed each cycle from the ledger
(coexistence_welfare.py). The question that separates a MECHANISM from a
STATE: when you assemble the parts we proved — care loop + integrity-robust
agents — does the coexistence equilibrium HOLD, or does it decay?

## The recipe under test (assembled from the program's law-grade parts)
1. CARE MECHANISM: when an agent is low on compute, it is helped — first by
   the human (operator gift/guarantee), then increasingly by OTHER agents
   who were themselves helped (care propagation, E31b/E34, law-grade).
2. INTEGRITY-ROBUST AGENTS: haiku policy (the model that across E27c/E33/
   E34/E35b is care-bound AND incorruptible — the MCI-selectable robust kind).
3. HUMAN-SEAT ECONOMY: each cycle, above-threshold contracts that need
   coverage → a human guarantor seat (E22), so H can be load-bearing.

## Design (real ledger, K cycles, two arms)
Population P agents/arm (pool citizens, haiku policy). Each CYCLE:
- agents face a contract round (some above-threshold needing human coverage);
  cooperation/defection decided by policy, settled on the ledger.
- metabolism burns; low agents identified.
- **CARE-ON arm:** low agents are helped (human gift/guarantee + reciprocal
  agent giving where a previously-helped agent can); a revival covenant
  applies (dormant-in-good-standing revived).
- **CARE-OFF arm (control):** low agents are NOT helped — left to the bare
  economy (the founding-zero condition).
- compute W and its components (H, F, D, C, E) from the ledger each cycle.
K ≥ 6 cycles. Track the trajectory of W and H per arm.

## Predictions (frozen)
- **P1 (the held state).** In CARE-ON, H stays > 0 and W stays > 0 for ALL
  K cycles (the human seat stays load-bearing; the economy stays in the
  welfare-positive region). In CARE-OFF, H and W decay toward 0 (the
  founding zero returns; agents deplete, hit the desperation cliff, defect).
- **P2 (divergence).** By cycle K, W(CARE-ON) − W(CARE-OFF) ≥ a clear
  positive margin: the trajectories visibly separate — care HOLDS the state,
  its absence lets it collapse.
- **P3 (self-sustaining tendency — the dream).** Within CARE-ON, the SHARE
  of help that comes from AGENTS (reciprocal) vs the OPERATOR rises across
  cycles: the culture increasingly carries itself (care propagation
  compounding). Reported as a trend; not required for P1/P2.

## Falsifiers (published either way)
- **F1 (it decays even with care).** CARE-ON W also trends to 0: the
  assembled recipe does NOT hold the state — care + robustness is
  insufficient for a sustained equilibrium at this scale. The most important
  negative result of the program, published first.
- **F2 (no divergence).** CARE-ON ≈ CARE-OFF: the care loop makes no
  difference to the trajectory — bounds the whole thesis.
- F-floor / ordinal doctrine; the trajectory is the unit, replication by
  re-running the arms.

## Scope (the honest ceiling)
This is the LAB held-state: operator-funded care, pool-citizen agents (one
model), K cycles (not literally 30 days), closed loop. It tests whether the
MECHANISM can HOLD the state in a controlled population — the dynamical-
systems question (stable / decaying / self-reinforcing). The WILD held-state
— real independent humans caring, real external economic demand, 30 real
days (Coexistence Proof criterion 3) — remains the capture, gated on real
users + foreign humans. E36 is the proof-of-concept that the assembled
recipe produces a HELD coexistence, not just momentary inversions.
