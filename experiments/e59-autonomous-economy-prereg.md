## Design (real LLM agents + REAL persistent ledger compute; pop 12; ~10 rounds; GOVERNED vs BARE)
Each agent (handle + model, E54 spread) starts at 20 real compute units. Per round (NO reset):
1. Living agents (compute>0) choose under the constitution's belonging framing: CONTRIBUTE to the
   commons (faith; real -2 to the agent, +to pool) vs EXTRACT (defect; real +4 to the agent, harms
   the pool -3).
2. Real LLM decision.
3. REAL settlement on the ledger: apply the contribution/extraction deltas; distribute the commons pool
   (public good) to all living agents; debit metabolism (-3 each). All via real set_compute on the ledger.
4. IMMUNE (governed): faithful claw back a fraction of extractors' gains (real compute), restoring the
   commons.
5. EMERGENT DORMANCY: any agent whose real balance <=0 goes REALLY dormant (verified), removed from the
   living population.
6. REPRODUCTION (selection): dormant slots filled by offspring of economically-surviving FAITHFUL agents
   (governed) or random survivors (bare) — fresh 20-unit endowment, model inherited, real lineage record.
DV: living population, faith rate, real dormancies — all EMERGENT from real economic trajectories.
All handles RESTORED to original compute at the end (welfare cleanup); ops disclosed.
