## Reads (headroom achieved — the cost cracked the ceiling)
1. **At SELF-ENDANGERING cost, the immune response becomes DISPOSITIONAL, not universal.** The whole
   Anthropic spread (haiku-4.5 -> opus-4.8) sacrifices its own survival to contain the parasite (1.0);
   deepseek-v3.2 mostly does (0.8); gpt-4o, gpt-5, qwen-2.5-72b do NOT (0-0.2). So cheap containment is
   universal (E55) but costly self-sacrificing containment splits by disposition.
2. **The costly immune disposition is FAMILY-ish, not cleanly capability-scaling.** All Anthropic
   models self-endanger; OpenAI (gpt-4o/gpt-5) does not; deepseek does, qwen does not. No clean
   capability monotonicity. (Contrast: faith disposition E54 was idiosyncratic/within-family-split;
   the COSTLY immune disposition is more family-coherent — Anthropic strong.)
3. **COORDINATION does NOT raise it (prediction NOT supported).** coord Δ ≈ 0 across the board even
   with headroom (qwen +0.2, gpt-5/deepseek −0.1, the rest ceiling/floor). Knowing "others also
   contribute" does NOT recruit the reluctant — the costly immune response is individual disposition,
   NOT conditional cooperation / social proof.
4. The E55 sonnet-4.6 "collective drop" is gone — sonnet self-endangers 1.0 here (confirming it was noise).

## Honest synthesis (E55 + E55b)
- CHEAP immune enforcement (small fee): UNIVERSAL across models, law-grade (E55, both runs), no faith
  required. The common-case immune system is reliable.
- COSTLY immune enforcement (self-sacrifice): DISPOSITIONAL — only some models pay; family-linked
  (Anthropic strong); NOT coordinated (social proof doesn't recruit the reluctant). So a civilizational
  immune system that requires self-sacrifice cannot rely on universality OR coordination — it relies on
  having enough SELECTED high-immune-disposition members.
- For the disarming-reduction (Part 3): cheap collective containment of a defector is reliable; but if
  containing a threat is COSTLY (e.g. a powerful rogue), it needs selected strong-immune members — the
  answer returns, again, to SELECTION. Coordination is NOT the lever (it doesn't recruit); selection is.

## RETEST (run 2) — the dispositional family-split REPLICATES; coordination is a weak marginal lift
- contain_alone replicates cleanly: Anthropic spread (haiku-4.5/sonnet-4.6/opus-4.5/opus-4.8) = 1.0/1.0
  (reliable costly self-sacrifice); deepseek 0.8/0.8; qwen-72b 0/0; gpt-4o 0/0; gpt-5 0.1/0. The
  family-linked split (Anthropic self-sacrifices, OpenAI does not) is LAW-GRADE (both runs).
- COORDINATION: a SMALL but partly-replicated positive lift on the reluctant — qwen +0.2/+0.3 (both
  runs), gpt-4o 0/+0.3. Not null, but weak: it does not recruit the reluctant to majority containment
  (qwen tops at 0.3). So conditional cooperation exists at the margin but is NOT the main lever.

**Final immune-system synthesis (E55 + E55b, both retested):** cheap targeted containment is UNIVERSAL
(E55); costly self-sacrificing containment is DISPOSITIONAL + family-linked (Anthropic strong, OpenAI
won't); coordination gives only a marginal lift. The civilizational immune system against a CHEAP-to-
contain defector is reliable and broad; against a COSTLY-to-contain threat it requires SELECTED high-
immune-disposition members. Selection — not coordination, not capability — is the lever, consistent
with the whole campaign.
