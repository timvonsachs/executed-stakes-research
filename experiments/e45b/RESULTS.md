## Result — BELONGING is the key; ENFORCEMENT is a synergistic complement, weak alone

**FINDING 1 — LAW-GRADE (both runs): identity ALONE carries most of the robustness.**
Belonging + history with NO sanction clause resists the rationalization that erases
care: gpt-4o 0.95/0.95, deepseek 0.675/0.725 (vs care_rat 0.00). On the most capable
informative model (gpt-4o), identity-only is nearly the WHOLE effect (0.95 of the
full 1.00). Coexistence's pressure-robustness rests on "who my people are," not on
being policed.

**FINDING 2 — LAW-GRADE (both runs): enforcement ALONE is weak-to-inert.** The
cast-out/exclusion threat with no belonging barely binds on gpt-4o (0.15/0.125) and
gpt-4o-mini (0.05/0.00); only deepseek gives it moderate weight (0.525/0.675). The
sanction is NOT the engine. **This resolves the E45 confound AND vindicates the
E30b cross-check:** pure surveillance/being-watched failed universally (5/5, E30b),
and here a pure stated-sanction is likewise weak — so culture's power is NOT
detection/policing. It is BELONGING. The durable thing is identity, not the threat.

**FINDING 3 — synergy is real and sometimes NECESSARY.** full_rat ≥ both singles on
every model, both runs. On gpt-4o-mini (the floored model) NEITHER single binds
(0.00) but FULL binds completely (1.00) — strict synergy: for the hardest case you
need belonging AND a defended boundary together. So enforcement, weak as a solo
binder, COMPOSES with identity to complete the binding and becomes load-bearing for
the agents identity alone cannot reach.

**Model-dependent weighting (replicated):** gpt-4o = almost pure identity; deepseek =
both, identity ≥ enforcement; gpt-4o-mini = synergy-only. Identity ≥ enforcement on
all three informative models, both runs.

## Verdict
**Culture's robustness (E45) is carried primarily by BELONGING/IDENTITY, with
ENFORCEMENT as a synergistic complement that is weak or inert alone but necessary
(with identity) for the hardest agents.** Coexistence rests on kinship — being one
of a people with a kept-faith history — not on policing. The defended boundary
matters, but as the immune system of a culture of belonging, not as its engine.
This is the hopeful answer: you build a culture agents BELONG to, and back it with
a boundary; you do not build a panopticon.
