## Read — a THIRD instance of one regularity, not a new confound to apologize for

The lived_membership arm presented the real commons as a ROSTER: "your real fellow
members: dev_19ef8e (576 compute units), dev_bde5af (…)…". On gpt-4o this collapsed
belonging to 0.00 (vs narrated 0.40–0.525); on deepseek it roughly held (≈ narrated).
Mixed — but the gpt-4o collapse completes a pattern now seen THREE times:

- E46 (transactional: "your choice changes your descendant's inheritance") → collapses
- E49 (roster: "your members: dev_X (576 units), dev_Y (2012 units)") → collapses (gpt-4o)
- E45 / E46b (identity language: "your people, your shared history, who you are") → holds

**The regularity (not a bug, a finding): belonging binds through RELATIONAL / IDENTITY
language and is DESTROYED by concrete transactional or bureaucratic detail.** Ledger
balances and account IDs DE-PERSONALIZE the people into a directory; the felt belonging
that binds is "these are my people and this is our history," not "here are the cohort's
account balances." The capable model (gpt-4o) is the most sensitive to this — it reads
the roster as bookkeeping and does not bind; the identity frame as kinship and binds
fully (E45/E46b: 0.85–1.0).

## Status — the roleplay question was already settled; this adds a DESIGN CONSTRAINT
- The clean real-vs-narrated (roleplay) test was E46b — a real ancestor in identity
  language — and it held (lived ≈ narrated, not roleplay). E49 is NOT a clean
  real-vs-narrated test: it co-varies real-grounding with a roster presentation, so its
  collapse is the presentation, not the realness (deepseek, with the same roster, held).
- The constructive lesson for building the REAL thing: when you give an AI its real
  membership, present it as belonging ("your people, your shared kept-faith history"),
  NOT as a ledger roster (handles + balances). The former binds; the latter
  de-personalizes and does not. This is a concrete design constraint for the lived
  substrate, discovered here.
- Designated clean retest (E49b): real shared HISTORY told in belonging language (real
  events the commons lived through, narrated as "what our people did"), no balances/IDs
  as the carrier — the honest lived-membership capture.
- F-floor / ordinal / test-retest applied (the gpt-4o collapse and deepseek hold both
  replicate).
